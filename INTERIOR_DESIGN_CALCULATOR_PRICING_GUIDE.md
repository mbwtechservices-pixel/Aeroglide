## How to Use the Calculator

1. **Click the "Interior Calculator" button** on the interiors.html page
2. **Step 1: Enter Your Details**
   - Full Name (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Phone Number (10 digits, required)
3. **Step 2: Select Your Preferences**
   - Choose number of floors (1-5)
   - Select your preferred plan (Basic/Gold/Premium)
   - Review material specifications
4. **Review Cost Summary**
   - See your total estimated cost
   - Cost updates in real-time as you change selections
5. **Submit Your Quote**
   - Click "Submit Quote"
   - Confirmation email is sent
   - Success summary displays with all details
